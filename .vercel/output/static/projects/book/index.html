About OpenShelf

OpenShelf is a free and open source online library dedicated to making knowledge accessible to everyone. We believe that access to books and information should be a universal right, not a privilege.

Our platform provides a modern, user-friendly interface for browsing, reading, and sharing public domain and openly licensed works. No paywalls, no registration barriers, no tracking.

OpenShelf respects reader privacy, supports multiple formats, and works seamlessly across devices. Every book, every person, everywhere.

Core Features

OpenShelf is designed with readers and communities in mind.

Universal Access

No accounts, no barriers. Start reading immediately with just a click. Works on any device with a browser.

Privacy Focused

We don't track your reading habits, sell your data, or require personal information. Your reading is yours alone.

Rich Collection

Browse thousands of classic literature, historical texts, academic papers, and openly licensed contemporary works.

Community Powered

Open source means anyone can contribute. Help us expand the collection, improve features, or translate the platform.

Open Source & Free Forever

OpenShelf is open source and available under the MIT license. Explore the code, contribute improvements, or deploy your own instance.
