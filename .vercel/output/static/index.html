About

Who are we?

Arcbase is more than just a typical software studio. We're a team, a movement, and a global community of enthusiasts. Together, we have a shared mission of bringing high-quality open source software to the everyday person. We work towards this goal by building professional-grade software, products, communities, and coalitions that challenge the existing tech status quo.

Our current focus is in the developer tools, kernel-level optimizations, and web services space. We take on any endeavor that furthers our goals of digital sovereignty and user empowerment. By removing the barriers to entry, we're making it possible for anyone, regardless of technical background, to build their digital life on a foundation they actually own.

Do we make iOS apps?

No. We don't play in walled gardens or proprietary ecosystems that restrict user freedom. We're exclusively focused on open ecosystems like Android and Linux. In our view, software should be transparent and accountable to the person using it, not the corporation selling it.

Principles

What we believe

Everything we build and ship is guided by a core philosophy of simplicity and ownership. We believe that when you understand your tools with clarity, you gain the confidence to innovate without fear.

Built for Clarity

We eliminate "black box" engineering. By keeping our source code open and our documentation accessible, we provide users with the clarity needed to audit their own security and privacy.

Shipped with Confidence

You should never have to wonder if an update will break your workflow. We rigorously test every tool we ship, ensuring you can move fast with confidence.

Community Owned

Arcbase is a coalition of developers and enthusiasts. Our roadmaps are shaped by the community, not by corporate boardrooms or advertising bottom lines.

Mission

The Open Source Mission

For too long, the digital world has been divided into walled gardens and proprietary "black boxes." We believe that true technological progress is only possible when the foundation is open, transparent, and owned by everyone. This isn't just about code—it's about the confidence of knowing exactly what your devices are doing.

Our focus on the Linux and Android ecosystems stems from a simple core belief: users should have the clarity to audit, modify, and improve the tools they use every day.
