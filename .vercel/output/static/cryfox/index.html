About

Browse without being watched

CryFox is a privacy-first Firefox fork that strips away telemetry, blocks trackers by default, and hardens your browser against fingerprinting. Built for users who value speed and security without sacrificing usability.

Most browsers track your every move. CryFox doesn't. We removed the telemetry, disabled the data collection, and added powerful privacy protections that work silently in the background.

Features

Built for privacy

Every feature is designed to protect your privacy without compromising experience.

Enhanced Privacy Protection

Strips away all telemetry and data collection. Your browsing history stays on your device, not in the cloud.

Tracker Blocking

Blocks known trackers by default. Built-in list updates automatically to catch new trackers.

Fingerprint Hardening

Randomizes fingerprint signals so advertisers can't build a profile on you across websites.

Performance Optimized

Without the tracking code, CryFox loads pages faster. Expect noticeably quicker browsing.
